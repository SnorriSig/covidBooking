Procedure for your COVID-19 test

Book an appointment
Log in on this page.
Then select the date and time for your test.
Before leaving home
Remember to bring a face mask and your yellow health card (‘sundhedskort’).
If you do not have a yellow health card, you must bring photo ID (passport, driver’s licence or identity card).
You should arrive as close to the scheduled appointment as possible to avoid the spread of infection.

Follow the guidelines from Danish Health Authority to protect yourself and others from infection.
See the guidelines
When you arrive
When you arrive, you must follow the instructions at the testing site.
You will then have the sample taken.
Test result
You can subsequently see your test result on sundhed.dk, in MinSundhed app or in MinLæge app.
You can also see your child’s test result on sundhed.dk
See your test result

When you have been tested for COVID-19, you can usually see your test result the day after you have had the test – and no later than two days after.
If you have not received an answer within two days after the test has taken place, you are encouraged to make an appointment for a new test.
